Welcome to Beyond Our Borders!

Traveling has always been a liberating and life-changing experience. I believe everyone should have the opportunity to see the world and immerse themselves in a cultural exchange of language, customs, and friendship. I have traveled to over 30 countries and documented my journey to share with you all. The memories I've made are invaluable and I hope I can make it easier for you to experience these moments too. Join me as push the boundaries and explore the world around me both inside and outside of the United States. I hope my journey can show you that there is beauty beyond the borders we create between each other both in the US and abroad!
